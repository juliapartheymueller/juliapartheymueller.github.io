Julia Partheymüller is a Senior Scientist at the Department of Government at the University of Vienna. At the Vienna Center for Electoral Research (VieCER), she coordinates the surveys of the Austrian National Election Study (AUTNES). She is also a member of the Research Network Data Science. Her research focuses on democracy and the dynamics of public opinion in Austria and comparative European contexts, with particular interests in electoral behaviour, political communication, survey methodology, and quantitative methods.

She previously worked as a DAAD Lecturer at the University of Essex and was part of the German Longitudinal Election Study (GLES) team at the Mannheim Centre for European Social Research (MZES), University of Mannheim. She holds a doctoral degree in the social sciences from the University of Mannheim and studied political science at Freie Universität Berlin and the University of Hamburg.
